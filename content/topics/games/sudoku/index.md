# Sudoku, from the basics forward

A complete, plain-English curriculum: every technique as a strategy you can act on,
in the order you'd actually reach for it.

## The one idea behind everything

Every technique answers one of two questions:

1. **What is the only digit that can go in this cell?** (cell-focused)
2. **Where is the only place this digit can go in this unit?** (digit-focused)

Harder techniques are just cleverer ways of shrinking the options until one of those
has a forced answer.

technique that works resets you to the top. Lessons 1 to 8 solve the vast majority of
puzzles.
