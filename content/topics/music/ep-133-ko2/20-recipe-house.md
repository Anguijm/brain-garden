# Chapter 20 — Recipe: House

Four-on-the-floor, hypnotic, built for movement. House is about a relentless kick,
an offbeat hat, and a groove that loops without getting old.

Assessment throughout; button steps are FACT from the linked chapters.

## Vibe, tempo, feel

- Tempo: about **124 BPM**.
- Grid: `1/16`. Swing: low to none (house is mostly straight; a touch of swing on
  the hats is optional).

## Group A — drums

Kick, clap (or snare), closed hat, open hat.

- **Kick:** steps **1, 5, 9, 13** — four on the floor, the heartbeat.
- **Clap/snare:** steps **5, 13** (beats 2 and 4), layered on top of the kick.
- **Open hat:** the offbeats, steps **3, 7, 11, 15**. This "tss" between the kicks
  is the signature house groove.
- **Closed hat:** optional 16ths for drive, lower velocity.

## Group B — bass

A short plucky bass on `GROUP B` in `key` mode. Classic house puts the bass on the
**offbeats** (steps 3, 7, 11, 15) so it bounces against the kick, or rolling 16ths
under it. Keep it simple and rhythmic.

## Group C — stab / chord

Sample or load a short organ or piano chord stab. Play it syncopated (try steps
**3, 11** and an extra push before the bar turns). One good stab carries a house
track.

## FX and mix

- **Sidechain** the bass (and the chord) to the kick ([chapter 11](11-mixing-and-master.md)):
  set the kick group as the source, the bass/chord groups as the destination. That
  pumping duck is core to the house feel.
- A bright **reverb** on the clap, a touch of **delay** on the stab
  ([chapter 10](10-effects.md)).
- Master compressor for cohesion.

## Make it pro

Record a slow **filter sweep** with the fader on the chord group across 8 bars
([automation](09-fader-and-automation.md)) to open the track up into a "drop." Use a
punch-in **high-pass** riser into the change and [resample](16-advanced-techniques.md)
it as a transition FX.

## Arrange

House lives on long, evolving loops. Build a main 8-bar groove, commit it, then make
scenes that add or remove one element at a time (drums only, add bass, add chord,
full, breakdown). Sequence them in [song mode](12-arranging.md) for a DJ-friendly
arrangement.
